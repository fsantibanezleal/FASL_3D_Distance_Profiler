# References

## Academic Papers

1. **Tomasi, C. & Manduchi, R.** (1998). *Bilateral Filtering for Gray and Color Images*. Proceedings of the IEEE International Conference on Computer Vision (ICCV). The foundational paper on bilateral filtering for edge-preserving smoothing.

2. **Paris, S., Kornprobst, P., Tumblin, J., & Durand, F.** (2009). *Bilateral Filtering: Theory and Applications*. Foundations and Trends in Computer Graphics and Vision, 4(1), 1-73. Comprehensive survey of bilateral filter theory and applications.

3. **Hartley, R. & Zisserman, A.** (2003). *Multiple View Geometry in Computer Vision*. Cambridge University Press. Reference for camera models, intrinsic/extrinsic parameters, and projection geometry.

4. **do Carmo, M. P.** (1976). *Differential Geometry of Curves and Surfaces*. Prentice-Hall. Mathematical foundations for surface curvature (Gaussian and mean curvature).

5. **Botsch, M., Kobbelt, L., Pauly, M., Alliez, P., & Levy, B.** (2010). *Polygon Mesh Processing*. A K Peters/CRC Press. Mesh generation and processing algorithms.

6. **Perlin, K.** (1985). *An Image Synthesizer*. SIGGRAPH. Procedural noise generation for terrain synthesis.

7. **Azinovic, D., Martin-Brualla, R., Goldman, D. B., Niessner, M., & Thies, J.** (2022). *Neural RGB-D Surface Reconstruction*. CVPR. Modern neural approach combining implicit surfaces with radiance fields.

## Standards

8. **ISO 4287:1997**. *Geometrical Product Specifications (GPS) -- Surface texture: Profile method -- Terms, definitions and surface texture parameters*. Defines Ra, Rq, Rz, Rsk, Rku roughness parameters.

9. **Whitehouse, D. J.** (2002). *Surfaces and their Measurement*. Kogan Page. Practical guide to surface metrology.

## Hardware References

10. **Intel RealSense D400 Series**. Stereo depth camera with active IR projection. 1280x720 depth, USB 3.0, 0.2-10 m range. https://www.intelrealsense.com/

11. **SICK Ranger3D**. Industrial 3D line-scan camera for conveyor-belt profiling. High-speed laser triangulation, up to 2560 px width. https://www.sick.com/

12. **Microsoft Azure Kinect**. Time-of-flight depth camera with 640x576 depth resolution, 0.25-5.46 m range. https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/products/kinect-dk/

## Software Libraries

13. **Open3D** (Zhou, Q.-Y., Park, J., & Koltun, V., 2018). *Open3D: A Modern Library for 3D Data Processing*. http://www.open3d.org/. Point cloud processing, surface reconstruction, and visualisation.

14. **Three.js**. JavaScript 3D library for WebGL rendering. https://threejs.org/. Used for interactive 3D surface visualisation in the browser.

15. **NumPy** (Harris, C. R. et al., 2020). *Array programming with NumPy*. Nature, 585, 357-362. Core numerical computation library.

16. **SciPy** (Virtanen, P. et al., 2020). *SciPy 1.0: Fundamental Algorithms for Scientific Computing in Python*. Nature Methods, 17, 261-272. Signal processing and image filtering.

17. **FastAPI** (Ramirez, S., 2018). *FastAPI: Modern, fast web framework for building APIs with Python*. https://fastapi.tiangolo.com/
